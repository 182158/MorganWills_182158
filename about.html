Morgan Wills

I'm a 27 year old British person that grew up in Amsterdam, The Netherlands. My experiences range from customer service to event management. I have a lot of experience in customer service due to my previous experiences with huge brands like Sky, Marlies Dekkers, and Logitech.

I studied Event Management in Rotterdam, and have a MBO and Business Tech diploma from Albeda. I am now currently a second year student in Breda University of Applied Sciences, studying Creative Business in International Media and Entertainment Management. At my study, I also run the programming department of the university radio: HUB Radio. Being part of the radio has also given me the chance to host a podcast, Perskeptive Podcast, and a radio show, The Great British Band Show.
